The components of the <b>PDELab</b> framework are the editors, tools and libraries which are used to define, solve and analyze PDE problems. <P> <b>PDELab</b> allows you to "wire-together" any combination of these basic components in a wide spectrum of configurations, from entirely generic in the nature of PDE problems that can be solved, to very specific for a given model of an experimental process.
