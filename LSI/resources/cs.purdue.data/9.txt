<head> <title>Bhargava Honored at Inventors Recognition Reception</title> <style type="text/css"> <!-- .style1 {font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif} div.figure1 { float: right; width: 30%; border: thin silver solid; margin: 0.5em; padding: 0.5em; } div.figure11 { float: right; width: 35%; border: thin silver solid; margin: 0.3em; padding: 0.3em; } --> </style> </head> <!--#include virtual="/include/header"--> <div align="center"> <table width="750"> <tr> <td><div align="center"> <h1 class="style1">Bhargava Honored at Inventors Reception</h1> </div></td> </tr> <tr> <td height="297"><p class="style1"><img src="images/002.JPG" width="225" height="375" border="10" align="left" />On Wednesday, November 2, 2011, the <a href="http://prf.org/">Purdue Research Foundation</a> hosted its annual Inventors Recognition Reception to honor Purdue researchers whose discoveries were patented during the 2010-2011 fiscal year.</p> <p class="style1">Among those being recognized this year was Computer Science Professor <a href="http://www.cs.purdue.edu/people/faculty/bb/">Bharat Bhargava</a> for his patent titled <a href="http://www.google.com/patents?id=-CnYAAAAEBAJ&printsec=abstract&zoom=4&source=gbs_overview_r&cad=0#v=onepage&q&f=false">Method and System for Providing Cellular Assisted Secure Communications of a Plurality of Ad Hoc Devices</a>.</p> <p class="style1">Credited to Professor Bhargava, Jeffrey D. Bonta, Hong-Yon Lach, and Xiaoxin Wu, the patent is for (according to its abstract) &quot;A method for providing secure communications among a plurality of ad hoc devices includes authenticating one or more first devices within a first network; authenticating one or more second devices within a second network; transmitting a group key to the authenticated first devices and to the authenticated second devices; establishing an ad hoc network by at least one of the authenticated first devices and at least one of the authenticated second devices using the group key; and communicating within the ad hoc network among the at least one of the authenticated first devices and the at least one of the authenticated second devices.&quot;</p> <p class="style1">Congratulations to Professor Bhargava on receiving this recognition!</p> <p class="style1">For more information, please see the article <a href="http://prf.org/news/purdue-research-foundation-reception-honor-university-entrepreneurs-researchers">&quot;Purdue Research Foundation reception to honor university entrepreneurs, researchers&quot;</a>.</p> </td> </tr> <tr> <td>&nbsp;</td> </tr> </table> </div> </body> </html>
