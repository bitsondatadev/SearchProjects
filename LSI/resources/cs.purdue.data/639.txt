<head> <title>Spafford Named Distinguished Fellow of ISSA</title> <style type="text/css"> <!-- .style1 {font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif} --> </style> </head> <!--#include virtual="/include/header"--> <table width="750"> <tr> <td><div align="center"> <h1 class="style1">Spafford Named Distinguished Fellow of ISSA</h1> </div></td> </tr> <tr> <td><div align="center"></div></td> </tr> <tr> <td><p class="style1 style1"><img src="images/09spafDistCRA.jpg" alt="Eugene Spafford" width="150" height="200" hspace="10" vspace="10" align="left" /><a href="http://spaf.cerias.purdue.edu/narrate.html" target="_blank">Professor Eugene Spafford</a> has been named a Distinguished Fellow of the <a href="https://www.issa.org/" target="_blank">Information Systems Security Association (ISSA)</a>. He earned this distinction for his leadership in foundational research in security technology, his leading role in development of influential educational programs, and his long-time advocacy and public service in information security. This is the highest honor given by the ISSA, and it follows Spafford's selection of the ISSA Hall of Fame in 2001.</p> <p class="style1 style1">Spafford is a professor in the Department of Computer Science and the founder and executive director of the Center for Education and Research in Information Assurance and Security <a href="http://www.cerias.purdue.edu/" target="new_window">(CERIAS)</a>. He is a fellow of the <a href="http://www.acm.org/" target="new_window">ACM</a>, the <a href="http://www.aaas.org/" target="new_window">AAAS</a>, <a href="http://www.ieee.org/portal/site" target="new_window">IEEE</a>, and of the <a href="https://www.isc2.org" target="new_window">(ISC)<sup>2</sup></a>. He has recently been recognized by the CRA with a Distinguished Service Award. He also received the Information Security <em>Security 7</em> A<span class="style1">ward, and the UPE Abacus Award.</span></p> <p class="style1">ISSA is an international association of information security practitioners who work to protect privacy, data and systems for businesses, government, education, health care and law enforcement.</p> </td> </tr> </table> </body> </html> </html>
