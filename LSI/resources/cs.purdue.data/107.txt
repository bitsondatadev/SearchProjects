</div> </div><br style="clear: both;" /> <div> <p> Dr. Xu received his Ph.D. degree in computer science and engineering in the Chinese University of Hong Kong. In 2007 and 2008, he was a visiting researcher at Michigan State University, East Lansing, where he worked on the problem of semi-supervised learning and kernel learning. He is currently a post-doctoral researcher in Purdue University, working with Prof. Alan Qi. His research interests include machine learning and its applications to social computing and bioinformatics. He has published over 20 papers in several top conferences and journals, such as NIPS, ICML, IJCAI, AAAI, ICDM, IEEE Transactions on Neural Networks, Neural Networks, and others. Dr. Xu serves as a PC member or reviewer for a number of conferences, such as Web Intelligence, the International Conference on Neural Information Processing, the International Conference on Pattern Recognition, the International Joint Conference on Neural Networks, the International Joint Conference on Artificial Intelligence, and others. He regularly servers as a reviewer for a number of journals, such as the Journal of Machine Learning Research, IEEE TNN, TKDE, Pattern Recognition, ACM TKDD, Neural Networks, and others. </p> </div> <div> <strong>Selected Publications</strong> <div style="margin: 0 0 10px 0;"> Zenglin Xu, Feng Yan, Yuan Qi, "Sparse Matrix-Variate t Process Blockmodels", <em>AAAI 2011</em>. </div> <div style="margin: 10px 0 10px 0;"> Feng Yan, Zenglin Xu, Yuan Qi, "Sparse matrix-variate Gaussian process blockmodels for network modeling", <em>UAI 2011</em>. </div> <div style="margin: 10px 0 10px 0;"> Zenglin Xu, Feng Yan, Yuan Qi, "InfTucker: t-Process based Infinite Tensor Decomposition", <em>CoRR abs/1108.6296</em>, 2011. </div> </div> <div> <span style="color: #777">Last Updated: October 25, 2011 05:06pm</span> </div> </div> </div> <div id="info"> <div id="contact"> <span class="header">Contact Information</span><br /> <p> Office: LWSN 2142Q<br /> Phone: 49-69382 </p><a href="&#109;&#97;&#105;&#108;&#116;&#111;&#58;&#122;&#101;&#110;&#103;&#108;&#105;&#110;&#64;&#103;&#109;&#97;&#105;&#108;&#46;&#99;&#111;&#109;">Send Mail</a> <a href="http://www.cs.purdue.edu/homes/xu218">My Homepage</a> </div> </div> </div> <div style="clear: both;" /> <div id="footer"> <div style="font-size: 80%;font-weight: 800;float: left;"> Computer Sciences, 305 N.
