-->2010 Distinguished Alumnus<!-- InstanceEndEditable --> </span> <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="FeatureContent" --> <p><strong><img src="Curtis Holmes.jpg" alt="" width="206" height="217" />Mr. Thomas &quot;Curtis&quot; Holmes Jr.</strong><br /> <em>M.S. 1986 </em>Computer Science</p> <p>Holmes brings more than fifteen years of executive leadership experience in providing innovative software solutions to global enterprises. Holmes joined Texert as President and Chief Executive Officer in April 2008.&nbsp; Prior to Texert, Holmes served as President and Chief Executive Officer of MetaSolv Software from July 2003 to December 2006, prior to MetaSolv being acquired by Oracle Corporation. &nbsp;Previously, he served as President and Chief Operating Officer from 2001 to 2003. He led MetaSolv&rsquo;s return to profitable growth through significant global expansion initiatives and key strategy-driven acquisitions, including acquiring operations support systems software assets from Nortel Networks early in 2002, and acquiring London-based Orchestream Holdings plc in 2003. Prior to joining MetaSolv in January 2001, Holmes was Vice President of the Intelligent Network Unit of Lucent Technologies. Under his direction, the Intelligent Network Unit achieved a major global expansion, market leadership position, and sustained revenue growth and profitability. He has more than 25 years experience in the communications software industry, including managing the development of operations support systems applications. He started his career at Bell Laboratories in 1984. Holmes is a director of Elanti Systems, Inc. and Greenhill School.&nbsp; He previously served as director of Tango Networks. Holmes earned an M.S. in Computer Science from Purdue University, and a B.S. in Computer Science from Southern University.</p> <!-- InstanceEndEditable --> <div style="height: 15px; width: 10px;"></div> <!-- InstanceEndRepeatEntry --><!-- InstanceEndRepeat --> <p><span style="color: #777">Last Updated: April 13, 2011 03:54PM</span></p> <div style="height: 15px; width: 10px;"></div> </div> <div id="info"> <!-- InstanceBeginRepeat name="InfoBoxes" --><!-- InstanceEndRepeat --> </div> </div> <div style="clear: both;"></div> <div id="footer"> <div style="font-size: 80%;font-weight: 800;float: left;"> Computer Sciences, 305 N.
