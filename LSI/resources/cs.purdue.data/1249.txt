Every week, one-two persons lead the discussion on a paper. The hope is that in time all those interested in networking can be abreast with cutting-edge (or perhaps the word is bleeding edge) papers in the area of networking. This seminar is open to all; however, if you are a regular attendee, you are expected to present at some point of time. During this semester, we will meet between 12:30 and 2pm on Fridays.
