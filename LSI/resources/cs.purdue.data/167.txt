-->2011 Distinguished Alumnus<!-- InstanceEndEditable --> </span> <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="FeatureContent" --> <p><strong><img src="2011/Capka.jpg" alt="" width="174" height="217" />Dr. David Capka</strong><br /> <em>M.S. 1973 &amp; Ph.D. 1983 </em>Computer Science</p> <p>David Capka has been named the 2011 Distinguished Alumnus for the Department of Computer Science.</p> <p>Capka is the Distinguished Technical Fellow and Chief Scientist for the Compass Project in Redondo Beach, Calif., at Northrop Grumman, (formerly TRW), and has been with the company since 1983.</p> <p>Many of his research projects and development contracts focus on payload management, defense information systems and border control.</p> <p>Dave has been a representative of the Computer Science Corporate Partners Program (CPP) since 2001. He first joined as a representative of TRW, and then Northrop Grumman after the purchase of his sector of TRW.</p> <p>His dedication to building a strong partnership with Northrop Grumman has resulted in support for scholarships, student groups, retention programs, and CPP membership at the Premier level. He has served as the moderator for the CPP for the past 1-1/2 years. His demeanor and familiarity with the department and CPP makes him a natural leader for the CPP. </p> <p>Capka received his BA in Mathematics at Cornell University, and his MS as well as PhD in Computer Science at Purdue. </p> <!-- InstanceEndEditable --> <div style="height: 15px; width: 10px;"></div> <!-- InstanceEndRepeatEntry --><!-- InstanceEndRepeat --> <p><span style="color: #777">Last Updated: April 13, 2011 02:23PM</span></p> <div style="height: 15px; width: 10px;"></div> </div> <div id="info"> <!-- InstanceBeginRepeat name="InfoBoxes" --><!-- InstanceBeginRepeatEntry --> <div> <span class="header"> <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="Info Title" -->Distinguished Alumni<!-- InstanceEndEditable --> </span> <!-- InstanceBeginRepeat name="InfoLinks" --><!-- InstanceBeginRepeatEntry --> <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="Link Text" --> <a href="2010/index.sxhtml">2010 Mr. Thomas &quot;Curtis&quot; Holmes Jr.</a> <!-- InstanceEndEditable --> <!-- InstanceEndRepeatEntry --><!-- InstanceBeginRepeatEntry --> <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="Link Text" --> <a href="2009/index.sxhtml">2009 Dr. Stuart Zweben</a> <!-- InstanceEndEditable --> <!-- InstanceEndRepeatEntry --><!-- InstanceBeginRepeatEntry --> <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="Link Text" --><a href="2008/index.sxhtml">2008 Dr. Daniel Reed</a><!-- InstanceEndEditable --> <!-- InstanceEndRepeatEntry --><!-- InstanceBeginRepeatEntry --> <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="Link Text" --> <a href="2007/index.sxhtml">2007 Dr. William Nylin</a> <a href="2006/">2006 Dr. Dorothy E.
