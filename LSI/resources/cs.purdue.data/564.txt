<!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="FeatureContent" --> <p>Thank you for your interest in the Computer Science Graduate Program at Purdue University. We are looking forward to assisting you with your application.</p> <p>&nbsp;</p> <p><strong>Fall Deadline is December 15th</strong> (main session of entry) </p> <p><strong>Spring Deadline is October 1st</strong></p> <p>&nbsp;</p> <p><strong>Application Steps</strong></p> <ol> <li>write a <a href="SoP.sxhtml">Statement of Purpose</a></li> <li>submit an <a href="electronic_application.sxhtml">Electronic Graduate School Application</a>: for an accurate evaluation of your application you must give the name of your Undergraduate Institution and list your overall GPA on a 1-4 scale.&nbsp; (Convert grade stated on your transcript, for example 90/100 becomes&nbsp; 90/100*4=3.6). <a href="electronic_application.sxhtml">more...</a></li> <li>pay the <a href="application_fee.sxhtml">Application Fee</a></li> <li>submit your <a href="transcripts.sxhtml">Transcripts</a></li> <li>ask your references to submit <a href="letters_recommendation.sxhtml">Letters of Recommendation</a> online</li> <li>enter your <a href="english.sxhtml">TOEFL, IELTS, or PTE Academic</a> scores into the online application and report to Purdue (if applicable)</li> <li>GRE scores are not required for admission and do not need to be submitted. U.S. citizens and permanent residents who want to apply for an NSF Fellowship in their first year of graduate studies are recommended to take the GRE. </li> </ol> <p><strong>Mail</strong> only <a href="transcripts.sxhtml">transcripts</a>, <a href="transcripts.sxhtml">proof of award of degree</a> (if applicable), and rank certificates (if available) to us at the address below before the deadline. Documents that arrive after the deadline will be processed as well but we cannot guarantee that they will be included in the <a href="process.sxhtml">review</a>. </p> <p><strong>Graduate Admission<br /> Department of Computer Science<br /> Purdue University<br /> 305 North University Street<br /> West Lafayette, IN 47907-2107<br /> USA </strong></p> <p><strong>Phone:(765) 494-6004<br /> FAX:(765) 494-073</strong>9 </p> <p><em>Omission of requested information or falsification in the application forms or supporting documents or any determination by a competent authority of academic or other dishonesty will be grounds for denial of admission, revocation of admission, or expulsion from the University.</em></p> <!-- InstanceEndEditable --> <div style="height: 15px;
