<head> <title>Sponsored Program Awards in CoS up 18 Percent</title> <style type="text/css"> <!-- .style1 {font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif} --> </style> </head> <!--#include virtual="/include/header"--> <table width="750"> <tr> <td><div align="center"> <h1 class="style1">Sponsored Program Awards in CoS up 18 Percent</h1> </div></td> </tr> <tr> <td>&nbsp;</td> </tr> <tr> <td><p class="style1">Sponsored research activity for 2008-09 in the College of Science has increased, both in terms of dollars and number of proposals awarded, according to new numbers released by university.<br /> <br /> Program awards in the college were $61.5 million in 2008-09, compared to $52 million in 2007-08, an increase of more than 18 percent. The number of awards increased by 8.3 percent during that periods, from 532 to 576. &nbsp;The overall growth includes an increase in Discovery Park-associated awards, from $8 million (26 awards) in 2007-08 to $11.9 million (42 awards) in 2008-09.<br /> <br /> Support from federal agencies grew from $45.6 million to $53.3 million, an increase of 17 percent. Corporate support grew 51 percent, from $3.9 million in 2007-08 to $5.9 million in the 2008-09 fiscal year.<br /> <br /> Sponsored programs are defined as research and educational activities funded at least in part by external sponsors with the expectation of a defined outcome.<strong><br /> </strong></p> </td> </tr> </table> </body> </html> </html>
