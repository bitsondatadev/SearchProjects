<head> <title>CS Student Receives Google Fellowship</title> <style type="text/css"> <!-- .style1 {font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif} --> </style> </head> <!--#include virtual="/include/header"--> <table width="750"> <tr> <td width="730"><div align="center"> <h1 class="style1">CS Student Receives Google Fellowship</h1> </div></td> </tr> <tr> <td><div align="left" class="style1"> <p><img src="images/09lixia.liu-sm.jpg" alt="Lixia Liu" width="106" height="154" hspace="10" vspace="10" align="left" /><a href="http://www.cs.purdue.edu/people/graduate_students/liulixia/">Lixia Liu</a> received a <a href="http://googleblog.blogspot.com/2009/05/best-and-brightest.html" target="_blank">2009 Google Fellowship</a>. She is one of 13 graduate students nationwide to receive the Google Fellowship in it's inaugural year. The awardees of this honor will receive paid tuition and expenses, an Android-powered phone, and a Google mentor. Other recipients of this award attend University of Washington, MIT, University of Illinois - Urbana Champaign, University of Maryland, Princeton, Cornell, Brown, Berkeley, Stanford, and Carnegie Mellon.</p> <p>Liu is a research assistant under the supervision of <a href="http://www.cs.purdue.edu/people/faculty/ci/">Prof. Zhiyuan Li</a>. She joined the department in 2006, and interned at Google in the summer of 2008. Her research interests include compiler analysis and optimizations and programming systems for multi-core system, especially memory bandwidth aware compiler optimization.</p> </div> </td> </tr> </table> </body> </html> </html>
