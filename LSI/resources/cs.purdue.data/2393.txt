This is an informal program where students who are new to the department may seek help from their peers, which, in some situations, may be more valuable than advice from academic advisors or other people. While this program is open to any graduate student in the department, we believe that it will be most useful for students in their first year. </p> <p> All <acronym title="Graduate Student Board">GSB</acronym> members act as mentors. These individuals represent diverse group with different backgrounds and working in different areas, and you can choose a mentor, contact him or her via email or phone, and talk to your mentor on any topic. If you wish to contact a <acronym title="Graduate Student Board">GSB</acronym> member who does research in your area, see the <a href="/gsb/officers" class="wikilink1" title="officers">officers</a> page. </p> <p> Each year the <acronym title="Graduate Student Board">GSB</acronym> holds a discussion with incoming students during orientation week. </p> </div> </div> </body> </html> </html>
