ITaP Central Services Down June 20-21

ITaP services will be down overnight June 20-21 for a scheduled upgrade. Due to a large server upgrade over the weekend, many centralized ITaP services, such as computing labs (and printing), Blackboard, Software Remote, some web pages, and Advance C/S, will be unavailable. 

Students, faculty, and staff accustomed to working on weekends won't have access to most ITaP computing resources during the weekend of June 20 and 21, from 8 p.m. Saturday until noon on Sunday.  This is not expected to have any direct impact on CS systems.

For more information visit http://www.itap.purdue.edu/newsroom/detail.cfm?newsId=193
