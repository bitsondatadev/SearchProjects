2005 CS Summer Camp Sign Up

 

Purdue University Department of Computer Science's annual middle school summer camp, held in June on the Purdue West Lafayette campus, provides fun and educational experience using computers.

Although there is growing availability and use of computers in K-12 classrooms, students tend to apply them in rather limited ways, and classroom activities tend to be restricted to navigating the Internet and to applying various educational software packages. To expand the ways that students interact with computers and computer science, the Department of Computer Science has developed a summer camp program for middle-school aged youth.

response to this limited exposure to computer science, the Department of Computer Science has developed a summer camp program for middle-school aged youth.

Specific objectives include exposure to careers in computer science, increasing problem-solving skills, building awareness of the importance of math and science in preparing for a career in computer science, and increasing awareness of our K-12 outreach services that are available. Additionally, a second level camp has been developed for students wishing to continue the pursuit of computer science knowledge.

Campers will be housed on Purdue's campus in Cary Residence Hall and registration fees cover all meals, “fun night,” admission to the recognition luncheon, and all materials. Activities are led by Mindy Hart , Purdue University Computer Science K-12 Outreach Coordinator and Judith Lewandowski, Assistant Professor of Instructional Technology, Indiana University South Bend.

The Beginner Camp runs June 19-22, and will focus on programming using Lego Robotics and Web Page design. Beginner campers should be comfortable using a computer.

The Advanced Camp runs June 22-25 and takes a more in-depth look at Robotics and web design. Campers should have attended a previous session of the Computer Science Summer Camp or have some computer programming and Web page design experience.

Parents who register their middle-school children before June 1st will receive a discounted rate of $275. Go online and visit www.conf.purdue.edu/begcamp to register your 6th, 7th, or 8th grader for the beginner camp, or register students in grades 7, 8, or 9 for advanced camp at www.conf.purdue.edu/advcamp . Parents wanting more information about the Computer Science Summer Camp should contact Mindy Hart at 765-494-7802 or email her at mhart@cs.purdue.edu .
