<p></p> <TABLE BORDER="0" WIDTH="800"> <TR> <TD width="786" ALIGN="CENTER"> <h1 class="style6"> Purdue ACM Callout</h1></TD> </TR> <TR><TD ALIGN="LEFT"> <h2 align="center" class="style6"><strong><br> Wednesday the 14th, 6-10 PM<br> ME 261</strong></h2> <p class="style6">The Purdue Association of Computing Machinery is holding its Fall callout on Wednesday the 14th, at 6 PM in ME 261. Free food and drinks will be provided. Following an ACM information session and an open ideas/requests forum, students will be given the opportunity to become a member of ACM for the 2005-2006 school year free of charge (no dues will be required this year). The remainder of the meeting (until 10 PM) will be a social gathering with refreshments, both tournament and casual video game play, and of course plenty of CS students to meet, greet, and discuss Computer Science topics with.<br> </p> <p class="style6">(flier can be found at <a href="http://web.ics.purdue.edu/%7Enelogan/acm.pdf" target="_blank">http://web.ics.purdue.edu/~nelogan/acm.pdf</a>)</p> <p class="style6">The ACM also have 2 tech talks coming up on September 20th and September 21st. To learn more, visit the Purdue ACM web site at <a href="http://acm.cs.purdue.edu" target="_blank">http://acm.cs.purdue.edu</a>.</p> <p class="style8">&nbsp;</p> </TD> </TR> </TABLE> </BODY> </HTML> </HTML>
