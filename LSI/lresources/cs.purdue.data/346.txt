Every week, one (or two) persons lead the discussion. In time, all those interested in networking and systems can be informed about cutting-edge research in these areas. The seminar is open to all; however, you are expected to present at some point (not everyone will present every semester). <br> <br> <span style="font-weight: bold;">Venue:</span> LWSN 1106<br> <span
