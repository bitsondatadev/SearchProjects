</span> <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="FeatureContent" --> <p><em>For the Ph.D. with a specialization in computational science and engineering,</em> the requirements are the same as for the <a href="/academic_programs/graduate/curriculum/doctoral.sxhtml">regular doctoral program</a> except that: </p> <ul> <li> <p><strong>The <a href="/academic_programs/graduate/curriculum/doctoral.sxhtml#PlanofStudy">plan of study</a> must:</strong> </p> <ul> <li> <p>include four <a href="https://engineering.purdue.edu/CSE/cse-courses.html">CS&amp;E core courses</a> other than CS <a href="http://www.cs.purdue.edu/academic_programs/courses/">501</a>. </p> </li> <li> <p>include four courses in a single application area outside Computer Sciences. Appropriate <a href="https://engineering.purdue.edu/CSE/cse-courses.html">CS&amp;E core courses</a> may be used to fulfill application area requirements. </p> </li> <li> <p>include five of CS<a href="http://www.cs.purdue.edu/academic_programs/courses/"> 502, 503, 514, 520, 565, 580, and 584 </a>with </p> <ul> <li> <p>at least two of CS 502, 503, and 565; </p> </li> <li> <p>at least one of CS 514 and 520; and </p> </li> <li> <p>at least one of CS 580 and CS 584. </p> </li> </ul> </li> <li> <p>CS 514 and 520 may also be used toward the <a href="https://engineering.purdue.edu/CSE/cse-courses.html">CS&amp;E core</a> requirements. </p> </li> <li> <p>list at least one half of the course credits&nbsp; in Computer Sciences. CS 501 may not be used. </p> </li> <li> <p>reflect a GPA of at least 3.25. Grades must be A or B. </p> </li> <li> <p>be approved by the CS&amp;E graduate committee (as well as by the Computer Sciences graduate committee and the student's <a href="/academic_programs/graduate/curriculum/doctoral.sxhtml#AdvisoryCom">advisory committee</a>). </p> </li> </ul> <p>&nbsp; </p> </li> <li> <p><strong><a href="/academic_programs/graduate/curriculum/doctoral.sxhtml#QualPart2">Part 2</a> of the qualifying examination will contain a component, determined by the Computer Sciences and CS&amp;E graduate committees, covering the application area. </strong> </p> <p>&nbsp; </p> </li> <li> <p><strong>The <a href="/academic_programs/graduate/curriculum/doctoral.sxhtml#Thesis">thesis</a> is done under CS 699. </strong></p> </li> </ul> <!-- InstanceEndEditable --> <div style="height: 15px;
