id="feature_title">PURDUE UNIVERSITY<br />Head, Department of Computer Science</span> <p>Applications are invited for the position of Head of the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University from dynamic individuals with creative vision. The department has 48 faculty members, with active research programs in algorithms, bioinformatics, databases, distributed and parallel computing, graphics and visualization, information security, machine learning, networking, programming languages and compilers, scientific computing, and software engineering. Department faculty are also heavily involved in University-wide multidisciplinary research. The department has implemented a strategic plan for excellence supported by the higher administration, and has state of the art physical facilities and equipment. Further information about the department can be found at <a href="http://www.cs.purdue.edu">http://www.cs.purdue.edu</a>. Information about the College of Science can be found at <a href="http://www.science.purdue.edu">http://www.science.purdue.edu</a></p> <p>The successful candidate will have an exemplary record of scholarly achievement, a history of funded research, and outstanding leadership qualities and administrative abilities. It is expected that candidates for this position will have an earned doctorate and experiences sufficient to merit appointment with tenure at the rank of Full Professor. The Head will work with the faculty towards further advancement of the Department towards excellence in research, teaching, and engagement. Qualified persons should submit a letter of application, a statement of research and teaching, and a complete curriculum vitae with addresses and email addresses of four references. Review of applications will begin December 1, 2011 and will continue until the position is filled. Send electronic applications, nominations and inquiries to:</p> <ul><li><a href="mailto:h-apply@cs.purdue.edu">h-apply@cs.purdue.edu</a></li></ul> <p>Hard copy applications can be sent to:</p> <ul> <li>Martin M. Baker, Vice President</li> <li>Baker and Associates LLC</li> <li>4799 Olde Towne Parkway - Suite 202</li> <li>Marietta, GA 30068</li> </ul> <p>A background check will be required for employment in this position.</p> <p style="text-align: center;font-weight: 800;"><em>Purdue University is an Equal Opportunity/Equal Access/Affirmative Action Employer fully committed to achieving a diverse work force.</em></p> </div> <div id="info"> <div> <span class="header">Related Materials</span> <a href="CS_Head_Description.pdf">CS Head Description</a> </div> </div> </div> <div style="clear: both;" /> <div id="footer"> <div style="font-size: 80%;font-weight: 800;float: left;"> Computer Sciences, 305 N.
