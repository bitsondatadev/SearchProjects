align="left" class="style6"><img src="images/bartolacci.jpg" width="116" height="165" align="left">Congratulations on a great achievement, Mary Jo! <p align="left" class="style6">Mary Jo Bartolacci, conductor of the Computer Science Building Campaign Celebration and “virtual groundbreaking,” was honored by the Council for the Advancement and Support of Education (CASE). CASE is an association of premier advancement professionals working in education. It is an international organization that assists alumni administrators, fund raisers, public relations managers, publications editors, and government relations officers by providing resources and training to aid them in their development goals. <p align="left" class="style6">Mary Jo was recognized at the CASE V conference in Chicago on December 14 in the Individual Event category for the Computer Science Celebration. Mary Jo was the Computer Science Director of Development at the time of the campaign celebration, but has since accepted the position of Director of Advancement for the College of Science. <p align="left" class="style6">The virtual tour shown at the campaign celebration was hosted by Purdue President Martin Jischke. Visit the <a href="http://www.cs.purdue.edu/building/" target="_blank">New Building Homepage</a> on the Computer Science website to view the virtual tour.
