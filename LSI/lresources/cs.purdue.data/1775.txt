What problem is addressed ? Any hypothesis?<br> 2. What is the underlying motivation?<br> 3. What method was employed to address the problem? Analytical? Empirical? Mixed?<br> 4. What solution has been obtained? Is the solution general or applies only to the case study that is reported? If there is an hypothesis, was it rejected? Accepted?<br> 5.
