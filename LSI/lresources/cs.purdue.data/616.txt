It also supports the Civil Air Patrol. Additionally, Air University manages the College for Enlisted Professional Military Education, which includes the Air Force Senior Noncommissioned Officer Academy and the Air Force Institute for Advanced Distributed Learning.&nbsp; Another important part of Air University is the Air Force Institute of Technology located at Wright- Patterson Air Force Base, Ohio</font></p> <p><font face="Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif">Spafford's research interests include issues of computer and network security, cybercrime and ethics, and the social impact of computing.
