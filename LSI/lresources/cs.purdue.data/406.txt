InstanceBeginRepeatEntry --> <span style="margin-top: 0px;" class="feature_title"> <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="FeatureTitle" --><em>Are You Smarter than <span class="style3"><span class="blue">YOUR</span></span> 5th Grader? </em><!-- InstanceEndEditable --> </span> <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="FeatureContent" --> <p class="style9">Register NOW for the next round of Are You Smarter than YOUR 5th Grader? </p> <p class="style9">Workshop Dates: January 9th, 23rd, February 6th, 20th, 2010</p> <div> <span class="style6"><span class="blue"><img src="smarter10.JPG" alt="Beginners Camp" width="180" height="330" class="right" longdesc="" /> </span></span> <h4 class="style6"><span class="blue">About The Program</span></h4> <p>Are You Smarter Than YOUR 5th Grader? is for students in grades 4-6 and their parents or guardians. The program allows students and parents to develop understanding and experience success in technology alongside each other. Space is limited, so register today.</p> <p>&nbsp;</p> <p>Thank you to Motorola Foundation Innovation Generation Grants for funding of this program! </p> <h4 class="style6"><span class="blue">What You Will Do</span></h4> <p><br /> Participants will engage in an <strong>ALICE Storytelling Challenge and Pico Cricket design studio</strong>. Separate topical meals will be conducted for parent and child groups. Parents will hear from guest speakers to inspire them to share the joy of technology with their children. Children lunches will engage them in CS Unplugged activities<br /> and exposure to Purdue specific activities. Participants should plan to attend all 6 Saturday workshop sessions. Sessions will be led by local K-12 teachers and supported by current CS and Purdue students. Each session will begin at 9:00 a.m. and end by 1:00 p.m.</p> </div> <div> <img src="smarter family.JPG" alt="Advanced Camp" width="223" height="191" class="left" longdesc="" /> <h4 class="style7"><span class="blue">Cost</span></h4> <p>The workshop is offered at <strong>no charge</strong>, but a $25.00 deposit is required to register.
