id="feature"> <span style="margin-top: 15px;" class="feature_title"> LWSN 1198 Cisco Telepresence Meeting Room </span> <p> 2 seat Telepresence conference table. </p> <ul> <li style="list-style-type:disc">Cisco Telepresence System 1000</li> <li style="list-style-type:disc">PAL 2.0 Wireless access</li> </ul> <h4>Notes</h4> <p> The LWSN Cisco Telepresence System is on the NLR Telepresence Exchange. We are able to communicate with Cisco Telepresence Systems on NLR and other exchanges, e.g. Cisco and TATA. Conference calls are assigned a bridge number that all parties call to join the conference. </p> <h4>Scheduling</h4> <p> This room can be scheduled by sending an email request to <a href="mailto:events@cs.purdue.edu?subject=LWSN Telepresence Reservation&amp;body=Please reserve LWSN Telepresence at"> events@cs.purdue.edu</a> </p> All meeting requests should be a minimum of 24 hours prior to the meeting time. Please provide the following information: <ul> <li style="list-style-type:disc">Date and Time (please include timezone)</li> <li style="list-style-type:disc">Contact information of requesting party</li> <ul> <li style="list-style-type:circle">Name</li> <li style="list-style-type:circle">Purdue Department affiliation</li> <li style="list-style-type:circle">E-mail address</li> <li style="list-style-type:circle">Phone number</li> </ul> <li style="list-style-type:disc">Names and contact information of individuals attending meeting</li> <li style="list-style-type:disc">The remote Cisco Telepresence location(s)</li> </ul> After we receive this information, a bridge number will be assigned for the event. We will provide this bridge number to you and all parties for which you provide contact information. All parties to the conference call the bridge number to join the conference. <h4>Canceling</h4> <p /> Please contact <a href="mailto:events@cs.purdue.edu">events@cs.purdue.edu</a> to cancel a scheduled meeting reservation. <h4>Schedule</h4> <p> <iframe src="http://www.google.com/calendar/embed?showTitle=0&amp;showCalendars=0&amp;mode=WEEK&amp;width=600&amp;height=600&amp;wkst=1&amp;bgcolor=%23FFFFFF&amp;src=565u779bpnlf1irnfv8gtu279s%40group.calendar.google.com&amp;color=%23B1365F&amp;ctz=America%2FNew_York" style=" border-width:0 " width="600" height="600" frameborder="0" scrolling="no">Your browser does not support iframes</iframe> </p> </div> </div> <div style="clear: both;" /> <div id="footer"> <div style="font-size: 80%;font-weight: 800;float: left;"> Computer Sciences, 305 N.
