How does your solution differ from prior effort ?</li> <li style="font-family: century gothic;">What are the main contributions of your work (typically 2-3 main contributions need to be elucidated!) ?<br> </li> <li style="font-family: century gothic;">What was the research methodology you have followed ? Did you design a new system ? Or, evaluate an existing one ? Are you simulating a new algorithm ? What are the metrics for evaluation ?<br> </li> <li style="font-family: century gothic;">What evaluation and experimentation have you conducted ? What were your goals ? What did you expect your evaluation and experimentation methodology would confirm or find ? Format it as a double column report on a 10pt font which is the typical for several major conferences such as Infocom. Please spell out the author names and affiliations just as a regular research paper. The following sections are required in the paper.<br> </span> <ul> <li><span style="font-family: century gothic;">Abstract</span></li> <li><span style="font-family: century gothic;">Introduction</span></li> <li><span style="font-family: century gothic;">Related Work</span></li> <li><span style="font-family: century gothic;">System/Algorithm Overview and description<br> </span></li> <li><span style="font-family: century gothic;">Evaluation Methodlogy and Results</span></li> <li><span style="font-family: century gothic;">Future Work ---&gt; COMPULSORY to list all the things you would have loved to do if you had more time to finish.<br> </span></li> <li><span style="font-family: century gothic;">Conclusion</span></li> <li><span style="font-family: century gothic;">References</span></li> <li><span style="font-family: century gothic;">Please also attach an appendix that describes the software that you have used. (Some level of detail for future users would be beneficial). Think somebody who can follow up on your work and try to make it easy for them to continue your work. <br> </span></li> <li><span style="font-family: century gothic;">Attach a tar.gz file with a readme about how to compile/use your simulation platform. Again, think about somebody who can take your work and continue working on it. If you have obtained special software, then please attach them as well. If you have fixed some bugs on existing software, please state that too. <br> </span></li> </ul> <br> <ul> <span style="font-family: century gothic;"></span> </ul> <span style="font-family: century gothic;"><br> <br> <br> </span></td> </tr> </tbody> </table> <span style="font-family: century gothic;"><br> </span> </body> </html> </html>
