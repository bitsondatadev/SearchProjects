<!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="FeatureContent" --> <p><strong>University and Departmental Fellowships</strong></p> <p>There are a number of Departmental and <a href="http://www.gradschool.purdue.edu/funding/">University fellowships</a> for which graduate students will be automatically considered. Several of these fellowships are intended for outstanding entering doctoral students and some are awarded on the basis of need to United States citizens and permanent residents. </p> <p><a href="http://www.purdue.edu/bursar">Tuition</a> for Purdue fellows is remitted. <a href="http://www.purdue.edu/bursar">Fees</a> for incoming fellows in fall 2011 are about $471 per semester. International students pay an additional $60 per semester.</p> <p>The University provides a grant that Purdue fellows may use toward the purchase of the <a href="https://www.purdue.edu/push/billingInsurance/insPlan.shtml">medical insurance plan</a> of their choice. The grant is equal to the subsidy provided for students with graduate staff appointments.</p> <p><em>All University fees and tuition are subject to change without notice</em>.</p> <p>&nbsp;</p>
