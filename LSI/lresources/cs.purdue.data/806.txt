<head> <title>Prof. Patrick Eugster participates in 2011 DARPA Computer Science Study Group</title> <style type="text/css"> <!-- .style1 {font-family: Arial, Helvetica, sans-serif} div.figure1 { float: right; width: 25%; border: thin silver solid; margin: 0.5em; padding: 0.5em; height: 25%; } --> </style> </head> <!--#include virtual="/include/header"--> <div align="center"> <table width="869"> <tr> <td width="861"><div align="center"> <h1 class="style1">Prof. Patrick Eugster Participates in 2011 DARPA Computer Science Study Group</h1> </div></td> </tr> <tr> <td><div align="center"></div></td> </tr> <tr> <td><p class="style1">Prof. Patrick Eugster has been selected for this year's DARPA <a href="https://cssp.ida.org/">Computer Science Study Group</a> (CSSG). The CSSG is a multi-year program which every year selects a dozen assistant professors in computer science.</p> <p class="style1">The participants are familiarized with Department of Defense (DoD) missions and challenges during the first year, and funded for up to four years by DARPA in collaboration with other governmental institutions to conduct research on technologies that have the potential to yield innovative and transformative computer science and technology advances which are of interest to the government.</p> <p class="style1">Prof. Eugster has been selected based on his knowledge in programming languages and distributed systems, and more specifically for his research on "A Methodology for Developing Complex Event-based Software". In the context of this ongoing work Prof. Eugster is conducting research on support for developing reliable and efficient large scale and dynamic distributed systems.</p> <p class="style1">&nbsp;</p></td> </tr> <tr> <td>&nbsp;</td> </tr> </table> </div> </body> </html> </html>
