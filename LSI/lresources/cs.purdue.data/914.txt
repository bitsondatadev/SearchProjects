Zamboni <P> Sponsor: <a href="coast.html">COAST</a> Laboratory sponsors <P> <img src="goldRule.gif" width=100%><p> The vulnerability database and vulnerability analysis project at COAST is involved in collecting and analyzing computer vulnerabilities for a variety of purposes, including the application of knowledge discovery and data mining tools to find non-obvious relationships in vulnerability data, the development of useful vulnerability classifications, and the development of tools that will help generate intrusion detection signatures from vulnerability information. We intend to construct a resource that will be of long-term use to others in the research community studying issues of system vulnerability and protection. <P> <img src="goldRule.gif" width=100%> </body> </html> </html>
