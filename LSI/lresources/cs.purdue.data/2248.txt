Users anywhere on the net can access it to solve a broad range of simulations for physical objects and processes. Users import the GUI from //ELLPACK and specify the partial differential equation problem in a high level, natural, interactive format. The "server" examines the problem and assists the user in selecting appropriate solvers and machines that are available on the network or provided by //ELLPACK itself.
