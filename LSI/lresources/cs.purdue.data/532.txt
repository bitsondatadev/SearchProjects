<HTML> <TITLE>PaCS Group Homepage</TITLE> <H1><IMG ALT="PaCS Group" ALIGN=MIDDLE SRC="pics/PaCS.gif"></H1> <HR> <DT><H2><IMG ALT="o " SRC="pics/bluedot.gif"> Description</H2> <DD> PacsLab research is experimental and multidisciplinary in nature. The emphasis is on methodologies and software tools for seamless, secure, scalable and fault-tolerant concurrent computing on heterogeneous networked computing platforms. Central to this effort is the notion of domain-oriented software support, with diverse application domains including particle-physics, combinatorial optimization and manufacturing systems. The parallel simulation of stochastic and time-stepped systems is a major component of this effort. The environments we target are heterogeneous networked platforms, including large scale, geographically distributed, collections of computers ranging from parallel processors and servers/supercomputers to (clusters of) workstations and specialized computing engines. We intend to develop enabling technologies and prototype frameworks for collaborative high-performance distributed computing and simulation that may be adapted and enhanced to deploy scalable and portable systems. We build upon our combined expertise in heterogeneous distributed processing and multithreaded fail-safe computing to provide enabling prototyping technologies for collaborative interactive systems. <DT><H2><IMG ALT="o " SRC="pics/bluedot.gif"> Projects</H2> <TABLE BORDER=6> <TR><TD ALIGN=CENTER><A HREF="http:spots.html"> <IMG ALT="ACES" BORDER=0 SRC="pics/spots_trans_small.gif"> </A></TR></TD> <TR><TD ALIGN=CENTER><A HREF="http:parasol.html"> <IMG ALIGN=LEFT ALT="ParaSol" BORDER=0 SRC="pics/parasol_trans.small.gif"> </A></TD></TR> <TR><TD ALIGN=CENTER><A HREF="http:aces.html"> <IMG ALT="ACES" BORDER=0 SRC="pics/aces.small.gif"> </A></TR></TD> <TR><TD ALIGN=CENTER><A HREF="http:eclipse.html"> <IMG ALT="EcliPSe" BORDER=0 SRC="pics/eclipse.small.gif"> </A></TR></TD> <TR><TD ALIGN=CENTER><A HREF="http:conch.html"> <IMG ALT="Conch" BORDER=0 SRC="pics/conch.small.gif"> </A></TR></TD> <TR><TD ALIGN=CENTER><A HREF="http:ariadne.html"> <IMG ALT="Ariadne Threads" BORDER=0 SRC="pics/ariadne.small.gif"> </A></TR></TD> <TR><TD ALIGN=CENTER><A HREF="http:arachne.html"> <IMG ALT="Arachne Threads" BORDER=0 SRC="pics/arachne_trans.small.gif"> </A></TR></TD> <TR><TD ALIGN=CENTER><A HREF="http:display.html"> <IMG ALT="Display" BORDER=0 SRC="pics/display.small.gif"> </A></TR></TD> <TR><TD ALIGN=CENTER><A HREF="http://segovia.cs.purdue.edu:7777/CMPI/cmpi.html"> <IMG ALT="CMPI" BORDER=0 SRC="pics/cmpi.small.gif"> </A></TR></TD> </TABLE> <DT><H2><IMG ALT="o " SRC="pics/bluedot.gif"> Principal Investigators</H2> <DD><A HREF="http://www.cs.purdue.edu/people/rego">Dr. Vernon Rego</A>, Professor <DD><A HREF="vita.pdf">VITA <DT><H2><IMG ALT="o " SRC="pics/bluedot.gif"> Sponsors</H2> <DD>ONR, DOE, ARO, NSF <DT><H2><IMG ALT="o " SRC="pics/bluedot.gif"> Researchers</H2> <DD><A HREF="http://www.cs.purdue.edu/people/jrramos">Jorge R.
