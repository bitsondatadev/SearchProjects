</span> <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="FeatureContent" --> <p><strong>Criteria</strong></p> <p>Our recommendation for admission is in general based on a combination of the following criteria: CS course background and major GPA, class rank, <a href="letters_recommendation.sxhtml">letters of recommendation</a>, your <a href="SoP.sxhtml">statement of purpose</a>, research experience, publications, and <a href="english.sxhtml">English Proficiency</a>. Most of the successful applicants had major GPAs above 3.7 on a 4.0 scale. We take the quality of the institution into consideration. PhD applicants are expected to show potential for independent research.</p> <p><strong>Degree</strong></p> <p>Applicants must have received a four-year bachelor's or equivalent degree before the term they intend to enroll into our program. </p> <p>Most applicants have a degree in computer science. However, we also consider <a href="requirements_nonCS.sxhtml">applicants from other fields</a> with suitable backgrounds.</p> <p>Applicants who begin a graduate program in computer science at another institution should complete at least a year in that program before applying to us. If the program is a master's program, we normally require completion of the program before registration at Purdue. </p> <p><strong>Key Requirements</strong></p> <ul class="spaced_list"> <li>Graduate students are expected to learn on their own whatever programming languages may be needed for the courses they take.</li> <li>Understanding of the foundational concepts of computer science.</li> <li>Familiarity with data structures and their implementations in different languages.</li> <li>An understanding of computer architecture, compilers, operating systems, the analysis of algorithms, networks, and programming languages.</li> <li>Knowledge of mathematics through differential equations, linear and matrix algebra, and numerical methods. Proficiency in a programming language such as Java, C, or C++.</li> </ul> <p>Applicants interested in the <em><a href="/academic_programs/graduate/curriculum/statistics_cs.sxhtml">joint master's program with statistics</a></em>, should also have: </p> <ul class="spaced_list"> <li>Advanced calculus.</li> <li>A strong foundation in statistical methods.</li> </ul> <p><strong><a href="english.sxhtml">English Proficiency</a></strong></p> <!-- InstanceEndEditable --> <div style="height: 15px; width: 10px;"></div> <!-- InstanceEndRepeatEntry --><!-- InstanceEndRepeat --> <p>Contact Us: <a href="mailto:grad-info@cs.purdue.edu">grad-info@cs.purdue.edu</a><br /> <span style="color: #777">Last Updated: July 21, 2011 01:34PM</span></p> <div style="height: 15px; width: 10px;"></div> </div> <div id="info"> <!-- InstanceBeginRepeat name="InfoBoxes" --><!-- InstanceBeginRepeatEntry --> <div> <span class="header"> <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="Info Title" -->Useful Links <!-- InstanceEndEditable --> </span> <!-- InstanceBeginRepeat name="InfoLinks" --><!-- InstanceBeginRepeatEntry --> <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="Link Text" --><a href="steps.sxhtml">Application Steps </a><!-- InstanceEndEditable --> <!-- InstanceEndRepeatEntry --><!-- InstanceBeginRepeatEntry --> <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="Link Text" --> <a href="english.sxhtml">English Proficiency</a> <!-- InstanceEndEditable --> <!-- InstanceEndRepeatEntry --><!-- InstanceEndRepeat --> </div> <!-- InstanceEndRepeatEntry --><!-- InstanceEndRepeat --> </div> </div> <div style="clear: both;"></div> <div id="footer"> <div style="font-size: 80%;font-weight: 800;float: left;"> Computer Sciences, 305 N.
