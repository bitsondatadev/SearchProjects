<HTML> <HEAD> <TITLE>Internetworking and Systems Research Laboratory</TITLE> </HEAD> <BODY BGCOLOR="WHITE"> <FONT COLOR="#CE9C33"><H2>Internetworking and Systems Research Lab</H2></FONT> <P> The Internetworking and Systems Research Lab houses equipment used for continuing research in the Internetworking, Xinu, and Crosspoint projects. This equipment includes several Sun workstations (including multiprocessors machines). In addition, the lab contains both processor and communication equipment for the Crosspoint project. This includes a set of Intel workstations that serve as routers to the wireless net, a FORE ATM switch used as a backbone net for the Intel workstations, and several PCs with wireless network interfaces. <P> <FONT COLOR="#ce9c33" SIZE="-1"> <A HREF="http://www.cs.purdue.edu/ar98" TARGET="_top"> <CENTER> 1998 <BR> Annual Research Report</A> <P> <A HREF="http://www.cs.purdue.edu" TARGET="_top">Department of <BR> Computer Sciences</A> </CENTER> </FONT> </BODY> </HTML> </HTML>
