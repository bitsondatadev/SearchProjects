InstanceBeginEditable name="FeatureTitle" -->The Richard &amp; Patricia Lawson Computer Science Building<!-- InstanceEndEditable --> </span> <!-- InstanceBeginEditable name="FeatureContent" --> <p align="left" class="calendar">The Lawson Computer Science Building (LWSN) consolidates the Purdue Computer Science Department from five buildings to two. The building provides 45 faculty offices, 55 teaching assistant offices, and 70 offices for research assistants. There are four classrooms, four instructional labs, five research labs, and a student activity center. The building also offers students a variety of interaction areas, and a deli-style caf&eacute; and espresso bar. The building and instructional labs within the building are open 6AM to 2AM, 7 days a week with the exception of Christmas (25 December) and Thanksgiving (4th Thursday of November).</p> <p
